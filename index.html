About College

ISET is committed to creating lucrative careers for our students by integrating theory and practice in education.

ISET aims to make every student an expert in their field of study by providing high-quality education and exposing them to the real-world problem-solving environment, with an emphasis on innovation, research, and excellence. At ISET, we work together to make education more accessible to everyone, irrespective of their social and economic backgrounds. We have an incredible faculty capable of delivering high-quality education and specializing in the professional and personal development of students.

We help students to advance their career by offering the most sought-after courses and certification programs. Led by an eminent team of lecturers, our faculty strongly strives to equip students to achieve their academic goals through our effective study programs. ISET guarantees that every program has been verified by the Board of Technical Education, the higher education governance body constituted by the Government of Kerala. With excellent faculty and cutting-edge education infrastructure, we continue to be the best college in Alappuzha, Kerala, India.
