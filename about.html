ABOUT ISET

ABOUT ISET

ISET is committed to creating lucrative careers for our students by integrating theory and practice in education. ISET aims to make every student an expert in their field of study by providing high-quality education and exposing them to the real-world problem-solving environment, with an emphasis on innovation, research, and excellence. At ISET, we work together to make education more accessible to everyone, irrespective of their social and economic backgrounds. We have an incredible faculty capable of delivering high-quality education and specializing in the professional and personal development of students.

We help students to advance their career by offering the most sought-after courses and certification programs. Led by an eminent team of lecturers, our faculty strongly strives to equip students to achieve their academic goals through our effective study programs. ISET guarantees that every program has been verified by the Board of Technical Education, the higher education governance body constituted by the Government of Kerala. With excellent faculty and cutting-edge education infrastructure, we continue to be the best college in Alappuzha, Kerala, India.

Our vocational education and training courses are designed to create hands-on professionals capable of delivering sustainable services and solutions that help our communities to lead easy and hassle-free lives. After the successful completion of the courses, students will be awarded their certificates and are officially certified to get placed in top companies or they can pursue a research program in their field of interest, or even take up higher education courses from top universities and colleges anywhere in the world.

We believe in developing a progressive educational culture and offering technology-enhanced higher education curricula for sharpening the learning abilities of students and make them industry-ready graduates. Apart from offering advanced courses and training programs for students, we also provide them with personalized guidance and support to make them the leaders of tomorrow!




ISO 9001:2008: A testimony to our quality

High quality of education and consistent processes and practices have always been the bed rock on which ISET has been created, developed and run. This institution has been awarded the ISO 9001:2008 certification in recognition of our quality policy , process and standards. This has been a very important event that has enabled us to practice, sustain and steadily improve quality aspects towards achieving ISET’s mission and vision for excellence.




Our Courses

Students may pursue our vocational education and training courses, which offer them outstanding career opportunities in various industries like Civil, Automobile, Electrical & Electronics, etc.

We mainly offer four types of academic courses under three different categories, KGCE, Diploma programs, training and certification programs, and distance education courses. As the top-rated and best college in Alappuzha, Kerala, India, our courses are meant to upgrade the technical skill set of students and help them get hands-on expertise in the latest technologies. Not just providing theory classes, we create hands-on technology experience for our students to improve their abilities and make them stand out in today’s competitive world.
