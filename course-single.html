Civil Engineering is one of the most sought-after courses that encourage students to take up a career in designing aesthetically pleasing and sustainable homes and buildings. In a modern and developing society, civil engineers have a critical role to play.

At ISET, we offer a two-year full-time Civil Engineering course for students who have passed their Senior Secondary Level (SSLC) from any of the authorized educational boards anywhere in India. The Civil Engineering graduate program offered at ISET is one of the KGCE- Kerala Govt. Certificate Examination in Engineering courses approved by the Board of Technical Education. This program deals with Technical Drawing, Building Constructions, Water Supply & Sanitary Engineering, Surveying & Leveling, AutoCAD, etc.

If you are interested in designing and constructing healthy buildings for communities and wide range of businesses, then the Civil Engineering course offered at ISET is the best option you can make.
